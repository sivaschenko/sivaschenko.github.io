About Me

I am curious and easily inspired developer. My curiosity makes me dive deeply into frameworks, discovering how functionality is implemented. The same curiosity leads me to all available meetups, at least somehow related to my stack of skills.

I am frequently visited by inspiration. Ideas of possible projects are comming up constantly, does not matter is it week day or weekend, am I energized or tired. However, my inspiration quickly goes out, if other people are not joining initiative. That's why I am always trying to share my ideas, and find similar to myself developers, curious and inspired, to work on something interesting togather.

I definately encourage open source projects, so I am trying to keep all my useful staff open source, and contribute to all open source projects I am using.

I love to get and provide :-p feedback, so any comment on my repository or blog post is a present for me. Also I adore hardcore technical discussions, expecially with a pint of beer, so if I attend any conference or meetup, be sure to meet me at the afterparty.
